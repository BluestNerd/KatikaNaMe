About Us

Revolutionizing Dance Digital Space

KatikaNaMe was born from a passion for a Kenyan dance enthusiast and a recognition of the challenges dancers face in showcasing their talent professionally. We saw the need for a platform that understands the unique styles, and stories of dance.

With the experience of dance, and technology in the Kenyan space, we have created a solution that offers a cutting-edge digital presentation.

Whether you're a traditional dancer preserving cultural heritage or a contemporary artist pushing boundaries, KatikaNaMe gives you the tools to present your work with the professionalism it deserves.
