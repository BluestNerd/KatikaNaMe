About KatikaNaMe

KatikaNaMe is a platform built for Kenyan dancers to take control of their talent and careers. We provide dancers with the tools to create professional portfolios, network with industry players, and showcase their work in the best possible way.

Why KatikaNaMe?

Dancers in Kenya and Africa often lack structured platforms to present themselves professionally. With KatikaNaMe, you can:

  • Generate and download a professional dance portfolio to send with audition tapes.
  • Access insights on making the perfect audition tape.
  • Advertise dance-related events and workshops for free.
  • Find dance jobs in Kenya, Africa, and beyond (coming soon!).
  • Discover and watch the latest top dance performances in Kenya and worldwide.

How to Make a Perfect Audition Tape

An audition tape can be your key to landing the perfect dance job. Here are some expert tips:

  1. Pick the Right Routine: Showcase versatility but stay true to your strengths.
  2. High-Quality Video: Use good lighting and a clear background to keep the focus on you.
  3. Keep It Short: Industry professionals don’t have time for long clips—30-90 seconds is ideal.
  4. Introduce Yourself: Name, style, and a few words about your experience make a big difference.
  5. End with Confidence: Finish strong and leave a lasting impression.
