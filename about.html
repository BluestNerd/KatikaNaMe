About KatikaNaMe

KatikaNaMe is a platform dedicated to empowering dancers and performance artists in Kenya. Our mission is to provide a centralized space where artists can take control of their talent and careers by creating professional portfolios, websites, connecting with opportunities, and showcasing their work to recruiters and event planners.
