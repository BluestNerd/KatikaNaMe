About KatikaNaMe

KatikaNaMe is a platform dedicated to empowering dancers and performance artists in Kenya. Our mission is to provide a centralized space where artists can take control of their talent and careers by creating professional portfolios, connecting with opportunities, and showcasing their work to recruiters and event planners.

What We Offer

  • Portfolio Generator: Create and share professional dance portfolios that you can send to recruiters, event planners, and potential collaborators.
  • Event Advertising: Events and shows can advertise on KatikaNaMe to reach a targeted audience of dancers and performance artists. Contact us to get started!
  • Future Plans: We’re working on adding a Jobs Section to mine the web for dance opportunities in Kenya, Africa, and beyond. Event planners, workshop organizers, and recruiters will be able to post jobs and opportunities for free.
  • Performance Hub: Our goal is to feature the latest and best performances in Kenya, across the continent, and around the world. Stay tuned for updates!

Join Us Today

Whether you're a dancer looking to showcase your talent, an event planner seeking performers, or a fan of dance, KatikaNaMe is the place for you. Together, let’s build a vibrant community that celebrates and supports the art of dance.
